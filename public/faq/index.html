Frequently Asked Questions
#

Printing Services
#

Are all of your images available for print or licensing? Yes, all of my images are available for both print and licensing. Feel free to reach out for more details on how to purchase or license specific images.
What types of prints do you offer? I offer a variety of prints, including fine art prints, canvas prints, and standard photo prints in various sizes.
How are the prints produced? All prints are locally produced, using high-quality materials and professional-grade printers to ensure vibrant colors and sharp details.
Can I choose custom sizes for my prints? Yes! Custom sizes are available upon request; however, the maximum print size depends on the image quality. For example, a 35mm analog image won't have the same maximum print size as a digital 40MP image. Please reach out for guidance on the best size for each image.
How do I place an order for prints? You can place your order by contacting me directly for specific requests until the webshop is online.
Do you offer framing services? I do not offer framing myself; however, if you’re nearby, I can help connect you with local artisans to get your project framed.

Portrait Photography
#

What are TFP shoots? And do you offer TFP sessions? Yes, I offer TFP, which stands for - Time for Print - where, both the photographer and the model agree to work together without payment. They are not working for nothing, instead they collaborate on a project to exchange services for edited images, benefiting both parties' portfolios.
What types of portrait sessions do you offer? I offer a range of portrait sessions, including individual portraits, family portraits, and professional headshots.
How long does a typical portrait session last? Most portrait sessions last between 1 to 2 hours, allowing enough time to capture a variety of poses and setups.
Can I choose the location for my portrait session? Absolutely! You can choose a location that is meaningful to you, whether it’s outdoors, in an urban setting, or in a studio environment.
How many images can I expect per shoot? Depending on the session's length and support used, you can typically expect around 5 to 20 final edited images from each shoot. Keep in mind that for 35mm film, I'll have 36 exposures per roll, while large format photography captures only one image per sheet. The type of support used will ultimately impact both the cost of the session and the number of images delivered.
How will I receive my portraits? Once your portraits are ready, you'll receive them via a secure online gallery, where you can download high-resolution files.
Do you offer retouching services for the portraits? Yes, all portraits include a basic level of retouching to enhance the final images while maintaining a natural look.
Do you provide RAW files? I never provide RAW files.

Events Photography
#

What types of events do you cover? I cover a variety of events but mostly indoor or outdoor sporting events and live music.
Do you cover weddings? I do not cover weddings, as there are already many talented wedding photographers out there who specialize in capturing those special moments. I focus on other types of events to provide the best experience for my clients.
How long do you stay at an event? I typically offer packages that range from a few hours to full-day coverage, depending on your needs.
Can I request specific shots or moments to be captured? Absolutely! Sharing your desired shots or moments in advance helps me ensure I capture everything that’s important to you.
